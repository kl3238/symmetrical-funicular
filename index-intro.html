Kan Lin

Kan is an AAD student from GSAPP (Graduate School of Architecture, Planning and Preservation) She has been working in the Architecture industry for 2 years and now pivots to be a UX designer with the knowledge inherited from the previous industry. She is concentrating on human-centered design, data visualization, and design for women. Prior to GSAPP, Kan tried different scales of design in her practice in China, including architectural design, interior design, urban design, graphic design, jewelry design, and packaging design.
